Learn life saving First Aid skills on your phone, your computer or an Oculus Quest


This is the project I have spent the last 3 years perfecting.
It has been a journey of discovery, not only in programming and development, but in developing a team and working to fulfill changing requirements.
The work isnt done yet though! As the field of medicine evolves, so does the information needed to be represented within this game. I look forward to making it better every day.


Game Engine: Unity 3D
Language: .Net C#


Features:

  • 31 First Aid Skills including; DRSABCD, CPR, Defibrillation and more
  • Available on iOS, Android, Web and Quest (by arrangement)
  • Results saved to the St John Learning Management System
