Blast through waves of enemies with a variety of weapons as you swoop and dive through complex cave systems



This project was my final submission for my Bachelor of Games Development at SAE Qantm. I had a lot of fun making it, and whenever I look back, I always wish I had time to make it again with the knowledge I have now.



Game Engine: Unity 3D
Language: .Net C#


Features:

  • 1st Person Shooter
  • Cubic Underground Adventure
  • 4 Weapon Types
  • 4 Enemy Types
  • More bloom than you ever actually wanted to see
